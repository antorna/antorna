Postdoctoral Researcher

I obtained my BSc in Chemistry in 2015 from the University of Valencia. Then, I moved to Tarragona, to work under the supervision of Professor Ruben Martin in the development of Nickel catalyzed carboxylation and amidation reactions, including the implementation of isotope-labelling techniques. During these years, I obtained a MSc in Synthesis, Catalysis and Molecular Design from the University Rovira i Virgili in 2016 and then a PhD in Chemistry in December 2020. In July 2021, I joined the group of Professor Eva Hevia at the University of Bern to work on cooperative bimetallic chemistry, with a focus on their catalytic applications. Outside the lab, I enjoy teaching and practicing step aerobics, singing and travelling.
